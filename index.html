What We Do

Our Complete
Service Range

Vexo Engineering delivers 10 specialised mechanical and structural services across industrial India — from precision pipeline systems to conveyor installation, shed construction, and skilled manpower deployment. Every service is backed by trained teams, disciplined supervision, and an unwavering commitment to safety.

Category 01 Pipeline Works 06 Services
01 — Pipeline Works
High Pressure Pipeline
Installation and fabrication of high pressure pipeline systems for process plants, petrochemical units, and utilities — with qualified welders, pressure testing, and full documentation.
02 — Pipeline Works
GI Pipeline
Galvanized iron pipeline installation for water supply, firefighting networks, compressed air lines, and general utility distribution — overhead and underground, all pipe sizes.
03 — Pipeline Works
MS Pipeline
Mild steel pipeline installation for industrial process lines, cooling water, compressed air, fire hydrant systems, and utility distribution — with welding, painting, and pressure testing.
04 — Pipeline Works
SS Pipeline
Stainless steel pipeline installation in SS 304 and SS 316 for process, chemical, pharmaceutical, and food-grade facilities — precision TIG welding, contamination-free execution.
05 — Pipeline Works
Fire Fighting Line Installation
Complete MS and GI firefighting pipeline networks — main headers, branch lines, sprinkler systems, hydrant networks, and hose reel stations compliant with fire safety standards.
06 — Pipeline Works
Mechanical Spares Supply
Flanges, fittings, gaskets, fasteners, valves, and pipe materials sourced to exact specifications — supporting maintenance schedules, shutdowns, and breakdown requirements on time.
Category 02 Mechanical & Structural Works 04 Services
07 — Mech & Structural
Erection & Fabrication Work
Structural and mechanical fabrication — pipe spools, platforms, vessels, tanks — plus complete erection of plant assemblies to exact engineering drawings with in-process quality checks.
08 — Mech & Structural
Shed Works
Industrial shed and factory building construction — structural steel fabrication, column and rafter erection, purlin and bracing installation, roofing and side cladding for pre-engineered and conventional structures.
09 — Mech & Structural
Mechanical Works
Equipment installation and alignment, rotating and static equipment handling, mechanical maintenance, breakdown support, and general plant mechanical works across diverse industrial environments.
10 — Mech & Structural
Conveyor Works
Installation and erection of belt, screw, and chain conveyor systems — structure fabrication, idler and pulley installation, belt tensioning, drive unit mounting, and full mechanical alignment before commissioning.
Additional Service
Mechanical Manpower Supply
Skilled and experienced mechanical workforce — supervisors, pipe fitters, welders (SMAW, GTAW, GMAW), riggers, helpers, and mechanical technicians — deployed for industrial operations, project execution, plant maintenance, and shutdown activities. PAN India mobilisation, flexible team sizes, pre-deployment safety briefing included.
